# Learning and Development

Harley is developing deeper AI engineering capability through structured practice and a Level 6 AI Engineering apprenticeship. The goal is practical depth: understanding where AI systems help, where they fail, and how to build software responsibly around those trade-offs.

This page should stay factual and current as the portfolio grows. It should not overclaim expertise, publish private coursework, or turn learning into a credential list detached from demonstrated engineering work.
